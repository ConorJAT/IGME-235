Project #3 Proposal - The Monster Pit

For my Project #3, I would like to expand upon the 'DOM Adventure!' starter and create a JavaScript game/experience, where the player must battle waves of enemies and bosses, slowly getting more difficult the more waves the player completes.

As the player persists through the onslaught, they must be constantly watching their weapon use and inventory space, as each weapon has 'x' amount of uses and the player can only hold so much before their pack is full. The player will also have 3 lives, each with a certain amount of HP. Once all lives are lost, it's game over.

There will also be several types of enemies, which will span from different movement patterns to different attack patterns. Additionally, every xth stage, the player will encounter a boss in the player will really have to keep track of item usage.
