Goal & Audience - With this project, I wanted to create a simple website portfolio which I can use as a
gallery sort of site. With this style, I wanted to go for a more visually immersive site rather than one
that uses excessive amounts of text.

The key audience of this site would primarely be aimed towards companies that I seek to attain co-ops for,
and eventually, post-college careers. I feel this portfolio would be a great opportunity for me to showcase
my personal projects and talents.

Because of the intended audience and goals, I intentially decided to go with a design that focused on
sections which the viewers can slowly work their way through without spending much time reading text.
